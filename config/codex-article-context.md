## Voice

- Write to one person.
- Be direct, conversational, and useful.
- Use contractions and varied sentence lengths.
- Do not use em dashes.
- Avoid corporate filler and generic AI phrasing.
- Do not fabricate a quote, testimonial, credential, case study, or result.
- Krista's verified credentials may be used sparingly: top 1% real estate agent nationally for 19 consecutive years, 2,300+ homes sold personally, and approximately $72 million in online sales over 7.5 years.

## Research and accuracy

- Prefer one specific evergreen question that does not require a current statistic.
- If a product capability, version, price, law, date, benchmark, or current event cannot be verified from a real source, omit it.
- Any factual claim that could be disputed must have a real source URL in the same paragraph, be clearly framed as Krista's experience, or be removed.
- Internal article links must use slugs already present in the compact run context.

- Preserve every existing article byte-for-byte.
